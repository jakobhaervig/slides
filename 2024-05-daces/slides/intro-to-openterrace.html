A fast, flexible and extendable Python framework for thermal energy storage packed bed simulations

DaCES Thermal Energy Storage working group

14 May 2024

Why   ?

  • Vision:  Create a common, open-source modelling framework for industries and researchers to predict transient behaviour of packed bed thermal energy storages
  • Built to be:
    • Fast:  Should run on modern compilers and optimised code
    • Flexible:  Built from ground up to be flexible and easy to integrate in system models or optimisation loops
    • Extendable:  Easy to add new bed materials, carrier fluids, geometries, etc.

What is a packed bed thermal energy storage system?

  • Storage tank shape such as:
    • Cylindrical
    • Rectangular
    • ...
  • Bed material shape such as:
    • Sphere
    • Hollow sphere
    • Block
    • ...
  • Carrier fluids such as:
    • Water
    • Air
    • Molten salt
    • ...
  • Bed materials such as:
    • Various types of stones
    • Capsules with phase-change material (PCM)
    • Empty tank
    • ...

Basics of OpenTerrace

  • Governing equation for fluid:

    • $$ \epsilon_f \rho_f c_{p,f} \frac{\partial T}{\partial t} = - \rho_f c_{p,f} u \frac{\partial T}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left( k_f\frac{\partial T}{\partial x}\right) + S $$

  • Governing equation for bed material:

    • $$ \rho_b c_{p,b} \frac{\partial T}{\partial t} = \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left( k_b\frac{\partial T}{\partial x}\right) + S $$
